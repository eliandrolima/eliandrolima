## About me

👋 Hi! I'm **Eliandro Lima**, a Brazilian physician, full-stack developer, and healthtech founder working at the intersection of **Healthcare, Artificial Intelligence, and Software Engineering**.

With more than 15 years of clinical experience, I combine medical knowledge with technology to build solutions that make health information more accessible, organized, secure, and patient-centered.

### 🚀 What I'm building

I'm currently developing **ArkMED**, an AI-powered digital health wallet that helps people securely store, organize, search, and share their medical documents using natural language.

My work involves:

* Generative AI and NLP pipelines for medical documents
* Processing and structuring unstructured PDFs, images, and clinical records
* Semantic search, embeddings, and retrieval-augmented generation
* Patient-centered health data and consent management
* Privacy, security, and responsible AI applied to sensitive health data
* Web applications, APIs, and cloud-native architectures

### 🛠️ Technologies and interests

I'm especially interested in **digital health, clinical AI, health data platforms, open innovation, and patient-controlled medical records**.
